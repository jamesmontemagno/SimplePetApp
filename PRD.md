# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Pet-Friendly Venues Finder - MVP

### Document Information
- **Version:** 1.0
- **Last Updated:** September 27, 2025
- **Status:** Draft

---

## 1. Executive Summary

A web application that connects pet owners with pet-friendly venues and locations. The platform enables users to discover, share, and review pet-friendly establishments, creating a community-driven directory that helps pet owners find suitable places to visit with their animals.

---

## 2. Product Vision

To become the go-to platform for pet owners seeking pet-friendly venues, fostering a community where pet lovers can share experiences and help each other discover welcoming spaces for their pets.

---

## 3. Target Audience

### Primary Users
- Pet owners (dogs, cats, and other companion animals)
- Individuals actively seeking social activities with their pets
- New pet owners looking for pet-friendly spaces in their area

### Secondary Users
- Venue owners wanting to attract pet-owner customers
- Pet service providers seeking visibility

---

## 4. Goals & Success Metrics

### Business Goals
- Build a comprehensive database of pet-friendly venues
- Create an engaged community of pet owners
- Establish the platform as a trusted resource for pet-related venue information

### Success Metrics (MVP)
- 100+ venues listed in launch markets
- 50+ active monthly users
- 25+ user-submitted reviews
- Average session duration of 3+ minutes
- 70%+ user satisfaction rating

---

## 5. Core Features (MVP)

### 5.1 Browse Venues
**Description:** Users can discover pet-friendly venues in their area.

**Key Capabilities:**
- View a list of pet-friendly venues
- See basic venue information (name, location, type, pet policies)
- Filter venues by category (parks, cafes, restaurants, shops, etc.)
- Search venues by location or name
- View venue details including photos and amenities

### 5.2 Add New Venues
**Description:** Users can contribute to the platform by adding new pet-friendly locations.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Submit venue information (name, address, type, description)
- Specify pet-related amenities (water bowls, outdoor seating, leash policies)
- Upload venue photos
- Add contact information and hours of operation

### 5.3 Review & Rate Venues
**Description:** Users can share their experiences and rate venues.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Write text reviews
- Rate venues on a 5-star scale
- Rate specific aspects (pet-friendliness, cleanliness, amenities)
- View reviews from other users
- See average ratings and review summaries

### 5.4 Venue Detail Pages
**Description:** Comprehensive information page for each venue.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Display all venue information
- Show aggregated ratings and reviews
- Display photos submitted by users
- List pet-specific amenities and policies
- Show venue location on a map

---

## 6. Out of Scope (Post-MVP)

The following features are intentionally excluded from the MVP but may be considered for future releases:

- User accounts and profiles
- Social features (following users, friending)
- Event planning and meetup scheduling
- Direct messaging between users
- Mobile applications (iOS/Android)
- Real-time notifications
- Integration with mapping/navigation services
- Venue claiming by business owners
- Premium/paid features
- Multi-language support
- Advanced search filters (price range, distance radius)
- Booking or reservation capabilities

---

## 7. User Experience Requirements

### Usability
- Clean, intuitive interface requiring minimal learning curve
- Mobile-responsive design (works on phones, tablets, desktops)
- Fast page load times (under 3 seconds)
- Easy-to-use search and filtering

### Accessibility
- Readable fonts and appropriate contrast ratios
- Keyboard navigation support
- Screen reader compatibility for core features

### Content Quality
- Clear, concise venue information
- High-quality, relevant photos
- Accurate location data

---

## 8. User Stories

### Story 1: Discover Pet-Friendly Cafes
**As a** dog owner  
**I want to** find cafes where I can bring my dog  
**So that** I can enjoy coffee while spending time with my pet

### Story 2: Share a Great Experience
**As a** pet owner who found a great pet-friendly location  
**I want to** add it to the platform and write a review  
**So that** other pet owners can discover it too

### Story 3: Read Reviews Before Visiting
**As a** cat owner planning an outing  
**I want to** read reviews from other pet owners  
**So that** I can choose the best venue for my pet's needs

### Story 4: Understand Venue Policies
**As a** pet owner  
**I want to** see specific pet policies and amenities  
**So that** I know what to expect before visiting

### Story 5: Browse Local Options
**As a** new pet owner in a city  
**I want to** browse all pet-friendly venues near me  
**So that** I can discover places to take my pet

---

## 9. Content Requirements

### Venue Information
- Venue name (required)
- Category/type (required)
- Address/location (required)
- Description
- Pet policies
- Amenities list
- Photos (minimum 1 recommended)
- Contact information
- Hours of operation

### Review Information
- Star rating (required)
- Written review text
- Date submitted
- Optional: specific ratings (cleanliness, friendliness, amenities)

---

## 10. Assumptions & Dependencies

### Assumptions
- Users are willing to contribute content (venues and reviews)
- Pet owners actively seek online resources for pet-friendly locations
- Basic web application will meet user needs for MVP
- Users will access primarily via desktop and mobile browsers

### Dependencies
- Access to mapping/location services for displaying venue locations
- Image hosting capability for venue photos
- Community participation for content generation

---

## 11. Constraints

- MVP launch within 3-month timeline
- Limited budget for initial development
- Single platform (web-only, no native mobile apps)
- English language only for MVP
- Focus on specific geographic launch market initially

---

## 12. Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|--------|---------------------|
| Low initial content (venues) | High | Seed initial database with research; partner with local businesses |
| Insufficient user engagement | High | Implement simple onboarding; focus on user experience; encourage community building |
| Spam or low-quality content | Medium | Implement basic content moderation; community reporting |
| Competition from established platforms | Medium | Focus on niche market; emphasize community and pet-specific features |

---

## 13. Launch Criteria

The MVP is ready to launch when:
- [ ] All core features are functional and tested
- [ ] At least 50 venues are seeded in the database
- [ ] Platform is responsive on desktop and mobile browsers
- [ ] Basic content moderation is in place
- [ ] User testing with 10+ pet owners shows positive feedback
- [ ] Core user flows are smooth and intuitive

---

## 14. Future Considerations

While not part of MVP, the following should be considered for future roadmap:

1. **User Accounts:** Enable personalized experiences and saved favorites
2. **Mobile Apps:** Native iOS and Android applications
3. **Social Features:** User profiles, following, and social connections
4. **Events:** Pet meetups and organized gatherings
5. **Business Tools:** Venue claiming and management for business owners
6. **Advanced Search:** More sophisticated filtering and discovery
7. **Geographic Expansion:** Support for multiple cities and regions
8. **Monetization:** Sponsored listings, premium features, or advertising
